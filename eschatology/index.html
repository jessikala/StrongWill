Biblical Eschatology

Understanding the End Times through the Holy Bible

What is Eschatology?

Eschatology is the study of the “last things” according to the Holy Bible. It focuses on events such as the Second Coming of Jesus Christ, the resurrection of the dead, the final judgment, and the establishment of God’s eternal kingdom.

Key Scripture: Matthew 24, Daniel 12, Revelation 1–22

The Second Coming of Christ

Christians believe that Jesus Christ will return bodily, visibly, and gloriously to judge the living and the dead.

“For the Lord himself will come down from heaven…” – 1 Thessalonians 4:16

Signs of the End Times

  • Wars and rumors of wars (Matthew 24:6)
  • False prophets and deception
  • Increase in lawlessness
  • Gospel preached to all nations

The Rapture & Resurrection

The Bible teaches the resurrection of believers and transformation into glorified bodies to be with the Lord forever.

Scripture: 1 Corinthians 15:51–53

The Final Judgment

All humanity will stand before God. Those whose names are written in the Book of Life will inherit eternal life.

Scripture: Revelation 20:11–15

New Heaven and New Earth

God promises a new creation where righteousness dwells, and there will be no more death, pain, or sorrow.

Scripture: Revelation 21:1–5
